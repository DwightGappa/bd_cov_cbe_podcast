About podcast

This a podcast feed to easily listen to the .mp3 recordings created for the Covenant Church - Community Bible Experience Bible reading program. The podcast feed follows the Brookdale Covenant CBE reading schedule.

This podcast feed is voluntarily hosted by Dwight Gappa as for a way to give back the community at Brookdale Covenant Church.

Podcast feed has been tested and found working correctly on DoggCatcher on Android 4.4 through 6 and iOS 10.1 beta.

Feel free to contact Dwight Gappa with feedback through my email address (email address removed) or open an issue on Github project page https://github.com/DwightGappa/bd_cov_cbe_podcast.
